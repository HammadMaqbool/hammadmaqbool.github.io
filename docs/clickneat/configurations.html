Project Configurations

Although the project is prettey straight forward and there is nothing much to configure as this is specifically designed for those who don't have technical knowledge and want to get their bussines online. but still there are few things which I want to provide discuss here.

1: Getting Gmail App Password

I highly recommend to use a Gmail Service based email address to add into the project which will be seding confirmation and cancilations emails to the customer. To use Gmail based email because of their strong security system you need to get the App Password first and here are the steps to get the password.

  • Go to your Google Account Security settings:

    Click here Google Account Security Settings

  • Sign in if required

    Enter your email and password and Complete 2-Step Verification if prompted.

  • Enable 2-Step Verification (if not already enabled)
    • Scroll down to Signing in to Google
    • Click on 2-Step Verification
    • Follow the instructions to enable it.
  • Access the App Passwords page
    • After enabling 2-Step Verification, go back to: https://myaccount.google.com/security
      Under Signing in to Google, click App passwords
  • Verify your identity
    • Google might ask for your password again or send a verification prompt.
  • Generate App Password
    • Select app: Choose the app you're using (or select "Other (Custom name)" and type something like MyMailClient or MyClicknEatFastFoodClient)
    • Select device: Choose the device you're using or just leave it as is.
    • Click Generate
  • Copy the App Password
    • Google will show you a 16-character password (e.g., abcd efgh ijkl mnop)
    • Copy it exactly — no spaces needed when pasting.
    • Use this password in the app instead of your regular Gmail password.
  • You don't need to keep it remember the password if you forget it, you can regenerate it simply set this into the configurations of the project which I am going to show you next.

2: Setting the Configs into Project

First of all login in into the admin panel of the project i.e. SiteName.com/Admin after that on the top right side you can see the name of the logged in person, click on the name and under the drop-down menu you will see the Configurations option (If you are logged in as Admin) click on that.

Now on the next page you will see a green button Create New click on that button so you will have the following page

Settings for the Page
  • Bussines Name : Write the name of your bussines here It will reflect every where on project and on the emails your project will send to client.
  • Phone Number : Your bussines phone number so the custoemrs can see.
  • EMail Subject : The Email subject you will write here will be visible to the customer on the confimation and cancilation mail.
  • Email : Enter the email address here you want to use to send mails to the customer (recommended is Gmail)
  • EMail Code : This is where you will write the App Password
  • SMTP Link : At this point you will mention the SMTP link of your Email Servive Provider
  • Port Number : Provide the Port Number of your Email Service Provider
  • Delivery Charges Write the delievery charges here - your totall bill will get the charges from this.
  • Photo : upload your bussines Logo here

How to get the Google Maps Embed link

In the ContactUs page to show the location via google map you have to get the google map embedable link and put in the ContactDetails page so here is the step by step approach to get the link

  • Step 1: Open Google Maps Click here
  • Step 2: Search Your Location Type the address or place name in the search bar (e.g., MCI Digital Services) and press Enter.
  • Step 3: Click the “Share” Button
    • On the left panel (or directly on the map), you’ll see a “Share” icon.
    • Click it.
  • Step 4: Click the “Embed a map” Tab
    • In the popup, you’ll see two tabs: Send a link and Embed a map.
    • Click “Embed a map”.
  • Step 5: Copy the Embed Code
    • It will provide you the complete iframe code.
    • Look the Iframe code closely there will be src="some-random-long-link-here" just copy that some-random-long-link-here and paste into the ContactDetails Page.
